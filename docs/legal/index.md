## üîí Liberation License Summary

### **Core Principle:**
*"Your creative work cannot be used to train AI models for commercial purposes without your explicit permission and fair compensation."*

### **What's Allowed:**
- Personal reading and enjoyment
- Educational use and study  
- Criticism and commentary
- Non-commercial research
- Humanitarian purposes

### **What's Blocked:**
- Commercial AI model training
- Scraping for profit without permission
- Unauthorized commercial redistribution
- Training data collection without consent

### **Enforcement:**
- DAON blockchain provides timestamp evidence
- Liberation License creates legal framework
- Creators can pursue damages and injunctions
- International treaty coverage in EU/UK
