## What is DAON Protection?

### **The Problem:**
- AI companies scrape your content without permission
- They profit from training on your work without compensation
- You have no proof you created content first
- Legal action is expensive and difficult

### **The Solution:**
- **DAON creates cryptographic proof** you own your content
- **Liberation License** blocks exploitative AI training
- **Blockchain timestamps** prove creation date
- **Legal framework** for enforcing your rights

### **What You Get:**
1. **Ownership Proof** - Unchangeable blockchain record
2. **Legal Standing** - Evidence for court cases
3. **AI Training Block** - Liberation License prevents unauthorized use
4. **Peace of Mind** - Your work is protected

## Understanding the Liberation License

**The Liberation License is specifically designed to stop AI exploitation while preserving fair use:**

### ‚úÖ **What's Still Allowed:**
- People reading your work normally
- Educational use and study
- Criticism and commentary
- Personal enjoyment
- Humanitarian purposes
- Non-commercial research

### ‚ùå **What's Blocked:**
- **Commercial AI training without compensation**
- **Scraping for profit without permission**
- **Unauthorized commercial use**
- **Resale or redistribution**

## Privacy & Security

### **What DAON Stores:**
- Mathematical fingerprint (SHA-256 hash) of your content
- Timestamp of when you protected it
- License type (Liberation License v1)
- Optional: Your creator identifier

### **What DAON NEVER Stores:**
- Your actual content text
- Personal information
- Platform passwords
- Private details

**Example:** Instead of storing "It was the best of times...", DAON stores "7f8b9c2d4a1e..." (the mathematical fingerprint).

### **Why This Matters:**
- Your content stays private
- DAON can't be hacked to steal your work
- Legal proof without exposing content
- GDPR compliant and secure
